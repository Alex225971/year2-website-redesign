About Us

Located in the heart of Brighton, Pompoko is a cross between a restaurant, cafe and a takeaway. We have steered away from the typical Japanese sushi-style selections, instead, offering tasty Japanese dishes that satisfy both western and eastern taste buds alike. Our restaurant is a great place to go if you are looking for a fast and inexpensive bite to eat (without compromising on quality) before catching a film, having a drink at a nearby bar, or going to a gig or a play.

To get an idea of the tasty dishes we offer, please browse through our on-line menu.

We frequently have special dishes on offer, as our chef likes to experiment, creating exciting new dishes to add to our existing menu. Ask our staff for details.

Please let us know if you have any specific dietary requirements or food allergies.

FAQs

Do you offer a takeaway service?

Yes, you can pop into the restaurant and order anything to go!

Do you offer a delivery service?

No, unfortunately we do not currently offer delivery.

Can I book a table?

Unfortunately not, we currently operate on a first come first served basis. Sorry for any inconvenience.
