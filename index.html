Japanese Restaurant Brighton

Located in the heart of Brighton, Pompoko is a cross between a restaurant, cafe and a takeaway. We have steered away from the typical Japanese sushi-style selections, instead, offering tasty Japanese dishes that satisfy both western and eastern taste buds alike.

Our restaurant is a great place to go if you are looking for a fast and inexpensive bite to eat (without compromising on quality) before catching a film, having a drink at a nearby bar, or going to a gig or a play.

To get an idea of the tasty dishes we offer, please browse through our on-line menu and food gallery.
